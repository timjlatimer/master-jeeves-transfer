**Calling Protocol**: Wisdom Giants are called by either:
1. A council of the rooftop members, OR
2. The Project Manager on the rooftop

It is fractional HB1000 information from people who actually have experience in the relevant domain (e.g., Ruby Red's world — knowing it is expensive to be poor). It is the ability to connect back to the community you are actually working in, intellectually or geographically.
