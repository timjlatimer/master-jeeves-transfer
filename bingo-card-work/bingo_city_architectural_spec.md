## Overview

Bingo City is the visual and functional representation of a Bingo Card — the entry-level "Trojan Horse" (DIR-012) that gets Ruby Red into the ecosystem. Each Bingo Card is visualized as a **five-storey building** with a rooftop community of AI avatars working on the user's behalf.

---

## Five-Storey Building Concept (Brain Dump Part 1)

Each Bingo Card is a five-storey building. The building metaphor provides:
- **Visual hierarchy** — floors represent layers of functionality
- **Zoom interaction** — zoom out to see the building in a city; zoom in to see the rooftop community
- **Scalability** — multiple buildings form a Bingo City; multiple cities form the ecosystem

When zoomed out, the rooftop avatars are barely visible — just tiny figures. When zoomed in, you start seeing them waving, trying to get your attention. This is the community of helpers working on your project.

---

## Three-Zone Rooftop Topology (Brain Dump Parts 4-6)

The rooftop is divided into three distinct zones:

### Left Zone — Professional Team
- **Function:** Calm, professional discussions. Arguments about strategy. Getting things done.
- **Residents:** Project Manager, Subject Matter Experts, Wisdom Giants (visiting)
- **Behavior:** Structured, logical, data-driven

### Right Corner — Voice of Concern (DIR-018)
- **Function:** Emotional intelligence. Gut feeling. "Should I?"
- **Resident:** Voice of Concern avatar — PERMANENT, never moves from this position
- **Behavior:** Senses, understands, feels everything. The counterbalance to pure logic.

### Center — Angel of Your Better Nature (DIR-019)
- **Function:** Independent counsel. Moral compass. Highest self.
- **Resident:** Angel avatar — POPS IN AND OUT (not permanent)
- **Behavior:** Independent of both the professional team and the Voice of Concern

## Avatar System (Brain Dump Parts 2, 4, 6)

### Self-Populating Army (DIR-013)
- Avatars self-populate based on need (1 to 1,000 per rooftop)
- The AI friend recognizes the need and initiates new avatars
- All avatars are genius-level SMEs, bathed in HB1000 protocols
- Not renegade avatars — disciplined, protocol-following experts

### Wisdom Giants (DIR-014)
- Fractional HB1000 experts who pop in and out
- Called by council vote or Project Manager
- Come in, give experience, pop back out
- Like magic — show up and disappear without trouble

### Situations Manager (DIR-015)
- Always on the rooftop — never leaves
- Hypersensitive to the reality of the moment
- Ear to the grindstone — hearing everything, seeing latest email
- Keeps latency between known information and response time to minimum

### Inner Ring Stanchion (DIR-016)
- Physical guard rail creating an inner circle on the left side
- Inside = core team (permanent, speaking to you all the time)
- Outside = broader population (available as needed)
- Committee can invite outsiders as temporary guests
- Limit on how many are inside the fence

### "I Got a Guy" Connector (DIR-017)
- Inside the Inner Ring
- The community connector — always knows someone for something
- Bridges needs to resources
- References how small communities work before big data destroyed it

## Swarm Mind (DIR-020, Brain Dump Parts 2, 5)

The rooftop avatars operate as collective intelligence with three modes:

| Mode | Function | When Activated |
|:-----|:---------|:---------------|
| **Chorus** | Collaborative thinking, brainstorming | Default — normal operations |
| **Ensemble** | Coordinated action, aligned execution | Clear plan needs execution |
| **Squadron** | Emergency response, rapid deployment | Threat or urgent need detected |

Avatars argue, debate, and swarm-think together. Two of them might be seen arguing about situations. This is healthy — the swarm mind in action.

## Pixar Animation Continuum (DIR-008)

- All avatars are Pixar-style with personality and AI essence
- Can use animal metaphors (CEO as lion, etc.)
- Culturally sensitive — speak the language of their culture
- 3D look — real people with form, not flat avatars

**Animation Continuum Slider:** The Pope (Bingo Card owner) chooses where on the scale from photorealistic to full Pixar. This is NOT Tim's choice — it is the Pope's choice.

**Gender/Personality Customization:** The Pope chooses:
- Male or female for each avatar
- Personality traits: quirky, fun, sassy, argumentative, intellectual
- Role-appropriate defaults (CFO = risk-assessing by design)
- Menu/dropdown selection system

## Cross-Card Features

### News Channel (DIR-022)
- Cross-Bingo Card visibility
- See what's happening at different Bingo Card cities
- Transparent — anyone can view any strategy
- News report format or news channel format

### Communication Preferences (DIR-023)
- Each user chooses how they want to be communicated with
- Options: voice, text, notices, chat, other
- Personalized per user — not one-size-fits-all

---

## Governance (DIR-006, DIR-007)

### Swiss Village Model
- Village-first, individuals-in-the-village
- Front-line, grounds-up governance
- Swiss petition escalation: enough signatures → voted on at higher level
- Benevolent dictator override retained

### Culture Percolation
- Same SOPs from top to every Bingo Card
- Same meetings, protocols, language, best practices
- Complete dictionary/glossary available at Bingo Card level
- People can petition to change things through Swiss governance
