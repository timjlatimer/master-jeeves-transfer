# DIR-020: SWARM MIND — Three-Mode Protocol

### DIR-020: SWARM MIND — Three-Mode Protocol

**Status**: ACTIVE | **Date**: 2026-03-04 | **Source**: Tim HB1000 (Cloud Butterfly Part 2 & 5)
**Added**: 2026-03-04 (Horizon 1B — archaeological extraction)

The rooftop avatars operate as a **Swarm Mind** — collective intelligence that shifts between three modes depending on the nature of the threat or task:

| Mode | Function | When Activated |
|:-----|:---------|:---------------|
| **Chorus** | Collaborative thinking, open discussion, brainstorming | Default mode — normal operations |
| **Ensemble** | Coordinated action, aligned execution, structured teamwork | When a clear plan needs execution |
| **Squadron** | Emergency response, rapid deployment, crisis management | When a threat or urgent need is detected |

The swarm people on the Bingo Card take different roles in the swarm community and swarm mind depending on the nature of the threat. They might act in different swarm mind protocols or levels of organization. This must be embedded into the rooftop community design.

Avatars argue, debate, and swarm-think together on the rooftop. Two of them might be seen arguing about situations. This is healthy — it is the swarm mind in action.
