### DIR-003: BUILD PHILOSOPHY — Minimum Viable Distribution (MVD)

**Status**: ACTIVE | **Date**: 2026-03-04 | **Source**: Tim HB1000 (direct, Cloud Butterfly Part 8)

**Build 100% of the product — even 110%.** Then throttle the release, not the build. The full thing exists from day one.

This is **Minimum Viable Distribution (MVD)**, NOT Minimum Viable Product (MVP). MVP thinking builds the minimum and iterates. MVD thinking builds the maximum and controls the rollout. The product is never incomplete — only the distribution is staged.

| Approach | Philosophy | What Ships | What Exists |
|:---------|:----------|:-----------|:------------|
| **MVP** (old) | Build the minimum, ship it, iterate | Incomplete product | Only what shipped |
| **MVD** (new) | Build EVERYTHING, ship controlled version | Throttled release | 100-110% complete |

> MVD replaces MVP thinking across ALL projects, effective 2026-03-04.
