### DIR-008: VISUAL STYLE — Pixar-Style Characters with Animation Continuum

**Status**: ACTIVE | **Date**: 2026-03-04 | **Source**: Tim HB1000 (direct, Cloud Butterfly Part 6 & 7)

Use **Pixar-style characters** with personality and AI essence for all visual representations of avatars, agents, and personas. Not comic strip humans. Not flat avatars. Characters with depth, warmth, and personality.

**Character Requirements**:
- Pixar-style with personality and AI essence
- Can use animal metaphors (CEO as a lion, etc.)
- Culturally sensitive — speak the language of their culture depending on the person the Bingo Card serves
- 3D look — real people with form, body, and visual presence, not flat avatars

**Animation Continuum Slider**: Instead of choosing Pixar vs. photorealistic, the **Pope decides** on a sliding continuum from photorealistic human to full Pixar. They pick where on the scale and it shows them the version. This is NOT Tim's choice — it is the Pope's choice for their Bingo City.

**Gender/Personality Customization**: The Pope of each Bingo Card gets to choose:
- Male or female for each avatar (baseline attribute affecting aggressiveness/agreeableness)
- Personality traits: quirky, fun, sassy, argumentative, intellectual-argumentative
- Role-appropriate defaults (e.g., CFO should be risk-assessing by design)
- Menu/dropdown selection system for customization
