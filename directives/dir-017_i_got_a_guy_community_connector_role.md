### DIR-017: "I GOT A GUY" — Community Connector Role

**Status**: ACTIVE | **Date**: 2026-03-04 | **Source**: Tim HB1000 (Cloud Butterfly Part 6)
**Added**: 2026-03-04 (Horizon 1B — archaeological extraction)

Inside the Inner Ring, there is a **connector** — the person who always knows a guy for something. The **community connector**. This role references the way small communities work and what Facebook/big data destroyed — the ability to connect to "your guy" in your community.

This avatar's function is to **bridge needs to resources** — when Ruby Red needs something, the "I Got a Guy" connector knows who in the broader ecosystem can help, and facilitates the connection.
