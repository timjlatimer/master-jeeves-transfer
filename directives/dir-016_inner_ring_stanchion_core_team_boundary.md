### DIR-016: INNER RING STANCHION — Core Team Boundary

**Status**: ACTIVE | **Date**: 2026-03-04 | **Source**: Tim HB1000 (Cloud Butterfly Part 6)
**Added**: 2026-03-04 (Horizon 1B — archaeological extraction)

On the left side of the Bingo Card rooftop, there is a physical **stanchion/guard rail** creating an inner circle — the **Inner Ring**.

| Zone | Who | Function |
|:-----|:----|:---------|
| **Inside the ring** | Core team | Baseline setters. Speaking to you all the time. Permanent residents. |
| **Outside the ring** | Broader population | Available in different forms as needed. Called in for specific expertise. |

The committee can **invite outsiders in as guests** temporarily, but there is always a **limit on how many are inside the fence**. The Inner Ring maintains focus and prevents noise from overwhelming the core advisory function.
