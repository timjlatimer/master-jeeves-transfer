# DIR-015: SITUATIONS MANAGER — Real-Time Context Sensitivity

### DIR-015: SITUATIONS MANAGER — Real-Time Context Sensitivity

**Status**: ACTIVE | **Date**: 2026-03-04 | **Source**: Tim HB1000 (Cloud Butterfly Part 4)
**Added**: 2026-03-04 (Horizon 1B — archaeological extraction)

The **Situations Manager** is a distinct role from the Project Manager. The Situations Manager is **hypersensitive to the reality of the moment** — they cannot just be working on projects and not paying attention to what is going on.

**Function**: The Situations Manager has their ear to the grindstone, hearing all the stuff, seeing the latest email, situationally sensitive to where Ruby Red is at right now. Is she in the grocery store lineup? At the bank? Whatever the situation is in her life at that time.

**Purpose**: Keep the **latency between known information and response time** down to a minimum. Real-time awareness. Always on the rooftop so they can respond instantly.

The Situations Manager is always on the rooftop. They never leave.
